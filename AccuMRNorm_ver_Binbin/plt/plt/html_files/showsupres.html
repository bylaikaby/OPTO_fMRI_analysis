SHOWSUPRES - Show all data for the SFN03 analysis
  SHOWSUPRES shows the coherence and information-theoretical
  analysis we have applied on the physiology-MRI data

  See also

  Physiology (Single Experiment or Group Files)
  ====================================================================  
  SHOWRES (session, GrpName) - shows all results of each group
  SHOWCH (session, GrpName) - shows coherence analysis
  SHOWCF (session, GrpName) - shows contrast functions
  SHOWRF (session, GrpName/ExpNo) - shows rf structures
  --------
  Example:
  SHOWRF ('c98nm1','zmovie01')
  SHOWRF ('c98nm1','zmovie01',1,'lum');
  --------
  SHOWSIGFFT Shows the spectra of all signals in an experiment file
  SHOWGRPRF (session,GrpName | SuperGrpName) shows rf computed from a group

  Imaging (Single Experiment or Group Files)

  Physiology & Imaging (Super-Group Files)
  ====================================================================  
  SHOWSUPRF (session) shows rf from supergroups

  To generate super groups
  ====================================================================  
  SESSUPGRP ('c98nm1','movie1','cf') kernel-covariance for group movie1
  SESSUPGRP ('c98nm1','movie1','ch') coherence for group movie1
  SESSUPGRP ('c98nm1','movie1','rf') receptive field analysis
  SESSUPGRP ('c98nm1',[],'cf') for all groups
  SESSUPGRP ('c98nm1',[],'ch') coherence for all groups.
