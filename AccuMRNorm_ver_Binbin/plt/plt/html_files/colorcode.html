COLORCODE - To change the colormap and its num of levels of the figure
  USAGE :    colorcode([colorname],[nlevels],[fighandle])
  ARGS :      'colorname': automn,bone,colorcube,cool,copper,flag,gray,hot,
                                                  hsv,jet,lines,pink,prism,spring,summer,white,winter
  NOTE :      Default 'nlevels' is 64 in Matlab, looks poor...
