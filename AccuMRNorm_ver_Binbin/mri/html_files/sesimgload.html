SESIMGLOAD - Append all Paravision 2dseq imagefiles of a Session
SESIMGLOAD(SESSION,EXPS,ARGS,LOG) - uses imgload to read, preprocess
(if defined in ARGS) and dump image data into MAT files also including
the neural data.
