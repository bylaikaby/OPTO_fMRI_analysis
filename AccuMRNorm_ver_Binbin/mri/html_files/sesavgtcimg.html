SESAVGTCIMG - Compute average of all groups defined in ROI.groups
  SESAVGTCIMG(SESSION) is different from sestcimg in that it
  computes the average of all images and all groups defined in
  ROI.groups, and appends this average in the tcImg.mat file. This
  super-average, named AVGTCIMG is only for obtaining the best
  possible anatomical detail, in case that the images are of lower
  SNR due to their very high spatio-temporal resolution.

  See also SESTCIMG
