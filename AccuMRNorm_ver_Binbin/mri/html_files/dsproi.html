DSPROI - Display ROI with name RoiName on slice "Slice"
  DSPROI(Roi) is used to display the rois defined with
  MROIGUI. Slice and RoiName must be defined, otherwise they are
  assumed to be Slice=1 and RoiName = "V1"

  ======================================================
  RoiDef =
            session: 'm02lx1'
            grpname: 'movie1'
                  exps: [1 16]
            anainfo: {4x1 cell}
          roinames: {3x1 cell}
                    dir: [1x1 struct]
                    dsp: [1x1 struct]
                    grp: [1x1 struct]
                    usr: [1x1 struct]
                    ana: [136x88x2 double]
                    img: [34x22x2 double]
                      ds: [0.7500 0.7500 2]
                      dx: 0.2500
                    roi: {1x8 cell}
                    ele: {[1x1 struct]  [1x1 struct]}
  ======================================================
