SHOWSFN - Summary of plots and images from the SFN2003 Data
  SHOWSFN (SupGrp,StmType) shows the coherence and information-theoretical
  analysis we have applied on the physiology-MRI data.
  SHOWSFN ('zmov','chcfGrps');
  SHOWSFN ('zpol','chcfGrps');
  SHOWSFN ('zspo','chcfGrps');
    
  AVAILABLE GROUPS AND EXPERIMENT TYPES:
  SupGrp      = (zmov,zpol,zspo,zrnd,zpls,zp6c)
  StmType    = (chcfGrps,winGrps,SuperGrps)
  ExpType    = (neu,mri), See also

  Physiology (Single Experiment or Group Files)
  ====================================================================  
  SHOWRES (session, GrpName) - shows all results of each group
  SHOWCH (session, GrpName) - shows coherence analysis
  SHOWCF (session, GrpName) - shows contrast functions
  SHOWRF (session, GrpName/ExpNo) - shows rf structures
  SHOWRF ('c98nm1','zmovie01')
  SHOWRF ('c98nm1','zmovie01',1,'lum');
  SHOWSIGFFT Shows the spectra of all signals in an experiment file
  SHOWGRPRF (session,GrpName | SuperGrpName) shows rf computed from a group
