DSPXCOR - Display xcorr maps and time series (the xcor structure)
  DSPXCOR(xcor) is used to display the activity maps and the time
  series of the activated voxels. The xcor structure has the
  following fields:

        session: 'm02lx1'
        grpname: 'movie1'
            ExpNo: 1
                dir: [1x1 struct]
                dsp: [1x1 struct]
                ana: [136x88x2 double]
                epi: [34x22x2 double]
              aval: 0.0100
                  ds: [0.7500 0.7500]
                  dx: 0.2500
                mdl: [1x1 struct]
                dat: [34x22x2 double]
            tosdu: [1x1 struct]
                pts: [1560x2 double]
          ptserr: [1560x2 double]%
